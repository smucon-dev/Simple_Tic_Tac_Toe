### A simple terminal based tic-tac-toe game ###

<span style="font-family: monospace">
---------<br>
| X O X |<br>
| _ O _ |<br>
| X O _ |<br>
---------<br>
</span><br>
Enter your moves like this: 

> *Row + SPACE + Column + ENTER*

For example:
> *1 2 + ENTER*

<span style="font-family: monospace">
---------<br>
| _ X _ |<br>
| _ _ _ |<br>
| _ _ _ |<br>
---------<br>
</span><br>
The first move is player 'X'.
